# Design System: UniAcademic 2.0
**Project ID:** 1476171998101118631

## 1. Visual Theme & Atmosphere
The design follows a premium, cinematic "Ultimate Dark Mode" aesthetic. It is high-contrast, professional, and dense, emphasizing clarity and academic authority. The atmosphere is sophisticated and state-of-the-art.

## 2. Color Palette & Roles
* **Academic Gold (#F59E0B):** Primary branding, active icons, focus states, and key action buttons.
* **Deep Space Navy (#020617):** Main application background and headers.
* **Slate Surface (#1A2235):** Primary card background and interactive surfaces.
* **Gunmetal Secondary (#1E293B):** Secondary inputs, toolbars, and inactive states.
* **High-Contrast White (#FFFFFF):** Primary text and headings.
* **Muted Slate-Blue (#94A3B8):** Secondary text, placeholders, and inactive navigation links.

## 3. Typography Rules
* **Font Family:** Inter / Space Grotesk.
* **Headers:** Semibold to Black, often uppercase with wide letter-spacing (`tracking-widest`) for navigation labels.
* **Body:** Clean, legible sans-serif for content.

## 4. Component Stylings
* **Buttons:** Sharp but subtly rounded (`rounded` - 4px). Primary buttons use Academic Gold background.
* **Cards/Containers:** Subtly rounded corners (`rounded` - 4px) with Slate Surface (`#1A2235`) backgrounds. Very subtle borders or shadows.
* **Inputs/Forms:** Gunmetal background (`#1E293B`) with muted borders.

## 5. Layout Principles
High information density with structured whitespace. Consistent use of 4px-based grid (Tailwind `p-4`, `p-6`). Horizontal and vertical lines are used sparingly for section separation.
